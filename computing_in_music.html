    • How Technology has positively impacted music

    Music distribution and accessibility

    Improvements to technology have majorly evolved the music industry. One of the main advances that skyrocketed the music industry was the digital improvements that allowed the users to share and download their music on music sharing platforms for free. This and other digital distribution platforms such as iTunes or streaming services like spotify did significantly decrease the revenue in the music industry however it made the music itself much more accessible so I would say it had a overall beneficial effect. Musicians can also perform for fans with livestream to reach audiences across the world which increases engagement of fans with their favourite artists with they may not be able to go and see live but in this it keeps the industry booming. Although it hurt the big guns in the music industry having cheap streaming services, it levelled the playing field which I belive was a necessary step to evolve the industry

    Creation of music

    For the Artists

    Tech allows musicians to record at a low cost and with ease for example at a home studio recording as this is now standardised. Music streaming services can also help clean up songs i.e. balance the dynamics etc. for people with a lack of experience in this. It can also reduce production time of songs as it can help the artists complete the tasks that aren’t based around the music itself

    Artificial Intelligence

    Technology has also evolved to be able to generate music using extensive databases of existing music, learning musical strucutres, harmonies, melodies, rhtythms, dynamics, timbres and forms to generate more music that matches the style of the material in the database to a simple prompt entered by the user. This could mean that companies could end up cutting out human artists out of the equation almost altogether meaning more money for the giant streaming services but this would be a less human approach to making music.

    Evolution of music

    Any great new music style could be determined by a new way of using tech, so AI should be viewed positively in this as it could create so much. I.e. needs electronics to get the electric guitar which would’ve meant no rock n roll. The Beatles used technology to speed up and slow down the tapes of the different instruments in the tracks they had recorded to match their pitch and tempo which was revolutionary at the time. Overall technology has been involved heavily in the evolution of music and it is not stopping! AI is the newest of the technology to enter and change the music industry. Whilst we went into more details of this in early sections of the page machine learning has allowed us to create high volume of trending music as well as new potential genres by mixing old genres with their vast databases which would definitely have a positive impact on music in general.
